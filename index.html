The "Inputs Lie" Research

A structural investigation into how deterministic ICS logic can be manipulated below traditional detection thresholds.

Inputs Lie: Trusting the Unverifiable in Critical Infrastructure

SCADA and ICS environments implicitly trust external signals — GNSS timing, sensor feeds, industrial protocol inputs — that can be manipulated by patient, sophisticated adversaries operating below conventional detection thresholds. This framework investigates the structural blind spot at the heart of most critical infrastructure security models.
